WELCOME TO
AHandling

The company was created by an interdisciplinary team working in the field since 1999. We use our experience in the new level handling services together with our partners AEROHANDLING, FAST LINE, SKY FOOD SERVICES. Private and corporate aircrafts, logistics companies, flights customers use our services. The final client for us is the Very Important Passenger. The crew is patronized by us from the moment of an order receipt. Being located in Borispol we work with the whole Europe, China, the Near and Middle East, the USA and Canada.

Our Services

  • Ground Handling Services

    We render a set of the airport, passenger and cargo-handling services, meeting the Customer and Crew orders. We provide quality control and completeness of the services rendered. In particular, we accompany and load the luggage, maintain the aircraft on blocks, and arrange communication between the apron and the cockpit. If necessary, we arrange maintenance, towing the aircraft at the aerodrome, keeping of aircraft repair kits, wheels and steers.

  • Fuel Support Services

    We offer the best price for aircraft refueling, with consideration of several fuel agents market offers. We store aircraft hydraulic fluids and lubricants.

  • Interior Deep Cleaning

    The service has been certified, allowing to perfectly cleaning corporate aircrafts with special antiallergenic odorless cleaning agents. We perform special types of cleaning. Now the cleaning takes place in Ukraine, not abroad, so you do not need to spend money for the ferry flight.

  • VIP servicing of the Very Important Passengers and the Crew

    You will be delivered to the plane with maximum comfort. You can use the advantage of emplaning through the Premier Hall or through the VIP-lounge.

  • Fast pre- and past-flight procedures

    By the Short Track principle: we will escort you to the plane in priority and quickly from the car park to the ramp and vice versa.

  • Hotel Accommodation and Transportation

    We arrange accommodation in the best hotels of 4* and 5* categories. You choose a place – and the rest is done by us: room booking, transfer arrangement. And you feel at home.

  • Catering by the Chef

    Order any dishes and the Chef from a certified company Sky Food Services will cook them for you, taking into account peculiarities of your national cuisine. We offer a menu for children, diabetics, vegetarians, etc. Cooking according to the modern technologies of hygiene standards guarantees safety of health.

  • Flight Operations Support

    We arrange a package of flight documentation and a crew briefing. Using our services your aircraft will arrive to any destination point on the planet at the declared time.

  • Additional services

    The Ukraine’s first certified washing of the corporate aircraft exterior using special equipment and certified detergents.

  • Service "Closer to home"

    It will take 20 minutes by helicopter for a Very Important Passenger to get from Kiev or the suburbs to Borispol and vice versa. Traffic jams are not a problem anymore: now you can accurately estimate your time and arrive on time.

  • All-Cargo Aircrafts Servicing

    We serve aircrafts of any class based on the “airplane - cargo terminal” principle. Trained staff use the handling equipment and mechanisms suitable for all types of cargo aircrafts, including all modifications of the Boeing-747-Freighter.

Why us/ Our advantages

  • Prevent nonscheduled departure and arrival delays

    The AHandling specialists monitor the timely provision of all services and prevent occurrence of painful surprises. We value your time.

  • Payment on a "pay-as-you-go" basis

    You pay for the services according to the invoice within three business days. Comfortable financial support services 24/7.

  • We guarantee comfortable prices
